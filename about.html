About Me

My Story

Hi, my name is Mina Okamura. I’m a UI designer, adept in supply chain management and photography. I’m constantly in awe of the art of craftsmanship, and I’ve always made an effort to be a contributor to that world in my professional career. After searching high and low for ways in which I can express myself creatively, I discovered that UI design was where I found my heart racing. Having worked in Tokyo, NYC and Los Angeles, I thrive to create visual language that is universal, sensible and inspiring - and that is MY craftsmanship.

As an experienced start-up manager in New York City as well as having run an online shop for my photography prints, I am capable of wearing every hat the customer needs. I am a team player, creative, and versatile in meeting project needs while building strong connections with colleagues and clients alike. Let me know how I can help add value to your design team!
